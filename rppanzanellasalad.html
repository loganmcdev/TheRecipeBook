Panzanella salad

(image)

Ingredients

  • Italian bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Dijon mustard
  • Red/White wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Dice the bread into bite sized pieces, toss in olive oil and lightly season. Arrange the bread on a sheet pan and bake at 400 degrees for 5 minutes or until lightly toasted, but still spongy.
  2. If using full size tomatoes dice roughly the same size as the bread, If using cherry tomatoes, slice in half. After slicing or dicing the tomatoes allow the juice to drain into a bowl and set the tomatoes aside.
  3. To prepare the basil, roll a bunch of fresh leaves and slice into a chiffonade.
  4. To create the vinaigrette combine the collected tomato juice, red wine vinegar, shallots, garlic, olive oil and dijon mustard.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the bread, tomatoes, basil, and vinaigrette, and toss to combine. Season the salad to taste, and allow it to sit for at least 15 minutes before plating.
