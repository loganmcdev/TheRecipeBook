Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Milk
  • Cocoa Powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. In a large bowl, combine: flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Mix until well-combined.
  3. Whisk together sugar and butter in a medium sized bowl until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time beating well after each. Stir in vanilla extract and the flour mixture in two parts, add milk and beat well.
  5. Using a greased muffin spoon batter into each spot 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 15-17 minutes. Once the tops are springy, and the toothpick comes out clean with a few moist crumbs.
  7. Allow cupcakes to cool in pan for five minutes before removing them, then transfer to a wire cooling rack to finish cooling.

Notes

Cupcakes are good stored at room temperature for four day, or refridgerated for five days.
