Caesar Salad

(image)

Ingredients

  • Romaine
  • Parmesan
  • Croutons
  • Caesar dressing

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl combine chopped romaine, grated parmesan, croutons and half the caesar dressing.
  2. Toss to combine, adjusting the texture as needed (If too dry, more dressing can be added; If too wet, add more parmesan).
  3. Once the salad has been mixed, place in a cool or room temperature bowl and finish with more croutons and shaved parmesan.
  4. Serve immediately for the best texture.
