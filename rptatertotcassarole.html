Tater Tot Cassarole

(Image of cassarole)

Ingredients

  • Hamburg
  • Tater Tots
  • Cream of mushroom
  • Cheese
  • Green beans
  • Sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F, cook hamburg on stove med-high stir for 7-10 or until completely browned.
  2. Pour beef into a 9x13" backing pan. Add in sour cream and spread evenly overtop (optional).
  3. Layer soup, green beans and tater tots evenly on top, sprinkle shredded cheese overtop.
  4. Bake in oven for 30-45 minutes or until taters tots are golden brown.
  5. Allow cassarole to cool before serving.

Notes

Adding sour cream adds a delicoious combonation of creamy richness, tangy flavor and a nice coolness.

you may use any size tater tots as long as they are arranged to cover dish.

for extra crispy tater tots, broil for a few minutes during final cooking stage.

(optional seaoning)while browning hamburger, add in 2 teaspoons of minced garlic,1 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning for extra flavor.
